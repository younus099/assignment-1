Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with marinated chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices. It is one of the most beloved dishes in South Asian cuisine and is enjoyed around the world.

1. Marinate the Chicken

   - Combine chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Marinate     for at least 1 hour.

2. Cook the Rice

   - Parboil basmati rice with whole spices like bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon.

3. Prepare the Base

   - Fry sliced onions until golden, then add chopped tomatoes, mint, and coriander.

4. Layering

   - In a pot, add marinated chicken, then layer with rice. Top with saffron milk and fried onions.

5. Dum Cooking

   - Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20‚Äì30 minutes.

6. Serve Hot

   - Mix gently before serving with raita or salan.

üìù Ingredients List:

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil or ghee
